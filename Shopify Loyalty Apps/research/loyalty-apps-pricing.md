## Key Differentiators

| App Name | What Sets It Apart | Best For | Biggest Weakness |
|----------|--------------------|----------|------------------|
| Joy Loyalty | Deep widget customization + stellar support + generous free plan | New and growing stores | Limited advanced analytics |
| Smile.io | Brand recognition + proven track record + advanced benchmarking | Small-mid DTC brands wanting reliability | Expensive paid plans |
| BON Loyalty | 250+ languages + built-in email + most generous free plan | International and omnichannel stores | Smaller ecosystem |
| Rivo | Full API/SDK + modern portal + fraud protection | Shopify Plus brands with dev resources | No free plan, Plus-focused |
| LoyaltyLion | Best-in-class analytics + segmentation + paid memberships | Established brands with marketing teams | High price, steep learning curve |

> **Note:** This differentiator data is used in the ["Which App Is Right for Your Store?"](../outlines/loyalty-apps-outline.md) section of the outline.
