## What Do Shopify Loyalty Apps Actually Cost?

Pricing across loyalty apps varies more than you'd expect — some let you start for free, while others jump straight to $199/month. That gap matters, so here's how the main options break down.

**Joy Loyalty** and **BON Loyalty** offer the most accessible entry points, with free plans and no transaction fees on either. BON gives you the most room to grow at up to 250 orders per month before requiring an upgrade, while Joy's paid tier starts at a reasonable $29/month. **Smile.io** has a free plan too, though it caps at 200 orders per month — and once you outgrow that, paid plans range from $49 to $999/month.

At the higher end, **Rivo** starts at $49/month with no free option and relies on usage-based pricing that can climb unpredictably as your order volume grows. **LoyaltyLion** is the steepest at $199/month minimum, built for established brands with dedicated marketing teams.

> **Bottom line:** If you're just getting started, a free plan from Joy, BON, or Smile.io is the smartest move. You can always upgrade once your program proves its value — no need to pay for features you haven't grown into yet.
