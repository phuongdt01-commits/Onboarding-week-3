You just launched your Shopify store, your first orders are rolling in, and now everyone's telling you the same thing: "You need a loyalty program." So you head to the App Store, filter by "free," and immediately hit a wall — one app caps you at 200 orders a month, another hides referrals behind a $49 paywall, and a third wants your credit card before you can even test the dashboard.

Here's the thing. You shouldn't have to pay $50 a month just to say "thanks for coming back" to your customers. And the good news is, you don't have to — at least not right away. Several Shopify loyalty apps offer genuinely useful free plans that go well beyond a basic points widget. The catch is that not all free plans are created equal, and picking the wrong one means you'll hit an upgrade wall weeks after launch.

We installed and tested five of the most popular loyalty apps on a live Shopify store to find out exactly what you get for free — and more importantly, where each app draws the line. Repeat customers spend up to [67% more per order than new ones](https://www.bain.com/), which means even a simple points program can pay for itself fast. But you need the right app to get there without burning through your budget before you see results.

In this guide, we'll break down the free tier of each app feature by feature, show you the real order caps and limitations, and give you a clear framework for deciding when (and whether) it's worth upgrading to a paid plan. No fluff, no affiliate rankings — just what we found after putting each app through its paces.
