## What Do Shopify Loyalty Apps Actually Cost?

Pricing varies more than you'd expect. Some apps let you get started for free, while others jump straight to $199/month — so it's worth understanding what you're paying for before you commit.

**Joy Loyalty** and **BON Loyalty** offer the most accessible entry points. Both have free plans with no transaction fees, and BON gives you the most room to grow with up to 250 orders per month before you need to upgrade. Joy's paid plan starts at around $29/month. **Smile.io** also has a free tier, but it caps at 200 orders per month — and its paid plans start at $49/month, climbing to $999/month at the top end.

On the pricier side, **Rivo** starts at $49/month with no free plan and uses usage-based pricing that can scale unpredictably as your store grows. **LoyaltyLion** is the most expensive option at $199/month minimum, designed for established brands with dedicated marketing teams.

The bottom line: if you're just getting started, a free plan from Joy, BON, or Smile.io is the smartest move. You can always upgrade once your program proves its value — no need to overspend on features you haven't grown into yet.
