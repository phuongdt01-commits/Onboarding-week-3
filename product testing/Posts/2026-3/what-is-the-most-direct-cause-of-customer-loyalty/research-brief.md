## 7/ Notes for Writer

### Causal Chain (use this as the spine of the article)
```
Product/Service Quality
        ↓
Customer Experience (all touchpoints)
        ↓
Customer Satisfaction (the most DIRECT cause)
        ↓
Customer Loyalty (behavioral + attitudinal)
```

**Customer satisfaction is the most direct cause** — it is the immediate antecedent that converts experience into repeat behavior and advocacy. CX creates satisfaction; satisfaction creates loyalty.

Critical nuance to include: a satisfied customer is NOT automatically loyal in ecommerce. Low switching costs mean satisfied buyers can still go to a competitor. The gap between satisfied and loyal requires a deliberate engagement system — which is what a loyalty program provides.
