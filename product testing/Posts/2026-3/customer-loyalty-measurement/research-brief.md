## H/ Notes for Writer

### Key Metrics to Define (All 8 Must Appear)

1. **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** — Formula: % Promoters (9–10) − % Detractors (0–6). Use case: Overall loyalty sentiment; compare across segments.
2. **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)** — Formula: Avg Purchase Value × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan. Use case: Predict total profitability per customer.
3. **Customer Retention Rate (CRR)** — Formula: (Customers at end − New customers) ÷ Customers at start × 100. Use case: Track how many stay (not how many arrive).
4. **Repeat Purchase Rate (RPR)** — Formula: Customers with 2+ purchases ÷ Total customers. Use case: **eCommerce-specific** — more actionable than retention % alone.
5. **Churn Rate** — Formula: Customers lost in period ÷ Starting customer count. Use case: Inverse of CRR; focus on "what we're losing."
6. **RFM Model** — Segment by Recency, Frequency, Monetary value. Use case: Identify high-value vs. at-risk segments.
7. **Behavioral vs. Emotional/Attitudinal Loyalty** — Framework: Behavioral (repeat purchases, frequency) ≠ Emotional (satisfaction, brand affinity). Use case: Understand *what* customers do vs. *why*.
8. **Customer Loyalty Index (CLI)** — Composite: NPS + Repurchase Likelihood + Satisfaction. Use case: Unified score for stakeholder reporting.

### Joy-Specific Angle to Weave In

- **Loyalty program metrics as direct measurement signals:** Referral link clicks, discount code redemptions, reward point velocity = real behavioral loyalty (cleaner than surveys).
- **Frame around "Assisted Orders"** — Joy's north-star metric. Show how to measure program-driven orders vs. organic; connect this to CLV & ROI.
- **Address the frustrated marketer:** "You have NPS data, but NPS doesn't tell you who bought again. Here's how to combine NPS with repeat purchase rate to measure *loyalty-driven revenue.*"
