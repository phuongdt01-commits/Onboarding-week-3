6. **Cost of Churn** — "Every lost customer costs more than you think"
7. **Referral & Advocacy Value** — "Your best marketing channel (word-of-mouth) dies when loyalty drops"

### CLV Formula to Include
**CLV = Average Order Value × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan**

Example for a Shopify store:
- AOV: $75
- Purchase frequency: 4x/year
- Customer lifespan: 3 years
- CLV = $75 × 4 × 3 = **$900**

Compare to CAC: If CAC = $45, then CLV:CAC = 20:1 (excellent).
If CAC = $300, then CLV:CAC = 3:1 (minimum viable).
