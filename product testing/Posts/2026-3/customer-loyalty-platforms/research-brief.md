## 8 Consensus Evaluation Factors

*(Extracted from 2026 competitor analysis + Perplexity consensus research)*

1. **Integration Capabilities** — APIs, Shopify, POS, CRM integrations (Very Common across 7+ sources)
2. **ROI Measurement & Analytics** — Dashboards showing revenue impact, retention lift, AOV increase (Very Common, 6+ sources)
3. **Implementation Speed & Ease** — Time-to-launch, no-code configurability, support during rollout (Very Common, 5+ sources)
4. **Data Security & Compliance** — GDPR, ISO certifications, audit trails, data privacy safeguards (Common, 3+ sources)
5. **Customer Support Quality** — Responsiveness, SLAs, onboarding help, issue resolution speed (Common, 3+ sources)
6. **Reward Flexibility** — Customizable earning/redemption (points, tiers, referrals, experiential) (Common, 3+ sources)
7. **Omnichannel Capabilities** — Unified earning/redemption across web, app, POS (Common, 3+ sources)
8. **AI-Driven Personalization** — Rule engines, segment-based offers, agentic AI (Emerging 2026 trend, 2+ sources)
