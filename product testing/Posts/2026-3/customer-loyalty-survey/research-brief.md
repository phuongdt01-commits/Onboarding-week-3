## SECTION 6: OUTLINE ROADMAP PREVIEW

**Likely H2 sections** (refined after outline approval):

1. **Why Customer Loyalty Surveys Matter (Churn Context)**
   - High CAC pressures merchants to focus on retention
   - Surveys = early warning system for at-risk customers
   - Tie to Joy: retention → Assisted Orders

2. **What to Measure: The Big Three Metrics**
   - NPS (likelihood to recommend)
   - CSAT (satisfaction with interactions)
   - CES (effort/friction in resolving issues)
   - ⚠️ When & why NOT to use each

3. **When & How Often to Survey (Without Fatigue)**
   - Interaction-triggered surveys (post-purchase, post-support)
   - Quarterly check-ins
   - Segmentation to avoid over-surveying
   - Cadence templates by merchant type

4. **Building Your Survey: Best Practices**
   - Question types (Likert + open-ended + multiple-choice)
   - Avoiding survey bias
   - Keeping it short
   - Mobile-first design

5. **Identifying At-Risk Customers (The Churn Signal)**
   - Spotting Passives (7-8 NPS) + Detractors (0-6)
   - What these scores mean for your loyalty program
   - Behavioral patterns that predict defection

6. **Closing the Loop: From Feedback to Action** ← **BIGGEST GAP / JOY FOCUS**
   - Immediate responses to Detractors (win-back)
   - Converting Passives → Promoters (loyalty incentives)
   - Systemic changes based on feedback
   - Integration with loyalty program optimization
   - Automation best practices
