## D/ Main Intent & User Context

### Search intent

**Primary:** Commercial evaluation — users are comparing loyalty management platforms to select the best fit for their business model (ecommerce, omnichannel retail, CRM-integrated).

**Secondary:**
- Integration research — how the platform connects to existing tech stack (CRM, POS, ecommerce)
- ROI justification — metrics and benchmarks to present to leadership
- Trend awareness — AI-driven personalization, first-party data, composable architecture

### User context (ICP) = Core user problems

- **Rising CAC:** Customer acquisition costs up 222% over 8 years in ecommerce (SimplicityDX); 60% increase in last 5 years across B2B/B2C
- **Customer churn:** Difficulty building long-term advocacy; loyalty fatigue from generic earn-and-burn programs
- **Data fragmentation:** Customer data scattered across CRM, POS, email, ecommerce — no unified profile
- **Manual management complexity:** Tracking points, tiers, and rewards manually is error-prone and time-intensive
- **Unpredictable revenue:** Can't forecast repeat business or identify expansion opportunities without analytics

### What this article MUST achieve

1. **Educate on evaluation criteria** — give readers a clear framework (7 factors) to compare platforms, not just a list of tools
2. **Address the scaling question** — unique angle: how to grow from basic points → tiers → referrals → advanced gamification
3. **Position Joy naturally** — not as a product pitch, but as the answer when the evaluation criteria point to ecommerce/Shopify needs
4. **Provide ROI justification** — arm readers with stats they can take to leadership
5. **Differentiate from listicles** — strategic depth, not "Top 10" format
