### H3: Factor #1 — Product Quality & Brand Trust

**Core insight:** High-quality products create a trust foundation. The metric: return rate. Customers with low return rates become confident referrers.

**Why it matters:** When return rate is < 5%, customers trust you enough to recommend you. This is the gateway to word-of-mouth.

**The mechanism:** Low returns → High confidence → Customers refer friends without hesitation

**Key metrics:**
- Return rate (benchmark: < 5% = healthy; > 10% = distrust)
- NPS referral likelihood (% who say "likely to recommend")
- Referral rate by return-rate cohort (do low-return customers refer 3x more than high-return customers?)

**Impact on Assisted Orders:** Low-return customers generate 2–3x more word-of-mouth referrals. This is your organic acquisition channel.

**Implementation:** Quality control + rapid refund/replace on issues. That's it—focus on the blocker.

*Joy placement: SOFT — Joy's post-purchase messaging can reinforce quality promise and surface returns for rapid response.*

### H3: Factor #2 — Personalization & Zero-Party Data

**Core insight:** Personalized offers drive repeat purchases faster because they're relevant. The metric: repeat purchase rate by segment.

**Why it matters:** Personalized segment gets 40–50% repeat rate vs. generic at 15–25%. That's the difference between loyalty and churn.

**The mechanism:** Asking preferences → Delivering what they asked for → Repeating faster

**Key metrics:**
- Repeat purchase rate: personalized segment vs. generic (benchmark: 2–3x difference)
- Email open rate by segment (personalized 40–50%; generic 25%)
- Referral rate by segment (do personalized customers refer more?)

**Impact on Assisted Orders:** Personalized customers repeat 2–3x faster, generating more Assisted Orders through repeat purchases + stronger referral likelihood.

**Implementation:** Post-purchase preference survey (one question: "What matters most to you?") → Segment → Deliver on it in next campaign.

### H3: Factor #3 — Seamless Post-Purchase Experience & Responsive Customer Service

**Core insight:** What happens after checkout determines if they come back—both through proactive engagement AND responsive support. The metric: time to first repeat purchase + support resolution rate.

**Why it matters:** Customers who get post-purchase attention repeat in < 30 days. Ignored customers don't return for 90+ days. More critically: customers with quick support resolution loyalty 3–4x longer. 96% say service determines loyalty.

**The mechanism:** Instant gratification (points earned immediately) + next-purchase prompts + responsive support (< 24-hour replies, issues resolved without friction) → Habit formation + trust → Faster repeat + confident referrals

**Key metrics:**
- Time to first repeat purchase after post-purchase sequence (benchmark: < 30 days = strong)
- Loyalty enrollment rate from post-purchase offer (benchmark: 25–35% when offered in confirmation email)
- Repeat purchase rate: engaged post-purchase customers vs. non-engaged (benchmark: 2x lift)
- Support response time (benchmark: < 24 hours = strong; < 12 hours = excellent)
- Issue resolution rate without escalation (benchmark: > 80%)
- Customer satisfaction (CSAT) on support interactions (benchmark: > 85%)
- Referral rate: customers with positive support experience vs. those who had issues unresolved (benchmark: 2–3x higher for resolved-well)

**Impact on Assisted Orders:** Faster repeats + trust from responsive service = more Assisted Orders from member repeat purchases + higher-quality referrals (customers confident in brand because support backs it up).

**Implementation:**
- Post-purchase automation: 3-email sequence (order confirmation → shipping update → "Your next purchase" incentive)
- Support infrastructure: Omnichannel (email + chat + SMS), < 24-hour response SLA, empowered team to resolve without escalation
- Service recovery: When issues occur, resolve visibly + offer loyalty recovery (bonus points, exclusive offer)

### H3: Factor #4 — Loyalty Program Design

**Core insight:** A habit loop (earn → return → refer) turns customers into salespeople. The metric: referrals per member.

**Why it matters:** Program members refer 5–10x more than non-members because referral incentives create a reason to come back + invite friends.

**The mechanism:** Habit loop (points → redemption → referral perks) + gamification (tiers, badges) → Members return frequently AND refer

**Key metrics:**
- Referral clicks per member (benchmark: 2–5% of members generate clicks monthly; > 5% = viral loop)
- Assisted Orders per member (benchmark: 1–1.5 referral orders per member/year is healthy; > 2 is exceptional)
- CLV lift: members vs. non-members (benchmark: 30–50% higher)

**Impact on Assisted Orders:** Program members directly drive Assisted Orders through two channels: repeat purchases (30–50% higher spending) + referrals (5–10x higher).

**Implementation:** Enrollment (post-purchase) + points (purchase + referral) + tiers (something to chase) + referral incentive (both parties).

## H2: Which Factors Drive the Most Assisted Orders for YOUR Store?

**Core insight:** Your product type dictates which factor activates first. All six matter—but start with your constraint.

**Product-type activation sequence:**

**Consumables (food, beauty, supplements):**
- Constraint: High churn from competitors offering same products
- Activate first: Quality + Personalization (repeat purchase rate is your survival metric)
- Then: Loyalty program (referrals from frequent buyers = virality)
- Expected lift: +40% repeat rate in 3 months; +50 Assisted Orders/month in 6 months

**Fashion/discretionary (apparel, home):**
- Constraint: Low repeat purchase rate; high price sensitivity
- Activate first: Ethical alignment + transparent pricing (build advocates, not bargain hunters)
- Then: Loyalty gamification (tiers make customers chase status, not discounts)
- Expected lift: +35% referral rate in 3 months; +30 Assisted Orders/month in 6 months

**Subscription/replenishment (recurring billing):**
- Constraint: Churn between renewal periods
- Activate first: Seamless post-purchase + personalization (engagement between orders)
- Then: Loyalty program with milestone rewards (keep them active until next order)
- Expected lift: +5% retention (each month) = +$10K–20K recurring revenue/month in 6 months

**Bottom line:** Identify your constraint (repeat rate, referral rate, or churn). That's where you start.
