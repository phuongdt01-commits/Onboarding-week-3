## D/ Main Intent & User Context

### Search Intent
**Dominant (70%):** Informational — strategic roadmap for building a loyalty program from scratch. Users want a complete, actionable framework, not just theory.

**Secondary intents:**
- Comparison: Which program type fits my business? (points vs. tiers vs. paid)
- Financial planning: What does it cost to run a loyalty program?
- Measurement: What KPIs track loyalty success?
- Tool discovery: What platforms and apps can help me build this?

### User Context (ICP) — Core User Problems
1. **The loyalty gap:** Executives believe customers are loyal; customers say otherwise. (61% vs. 20% — PwC)
2. **High CAC pressure:** Acquiring new customers is expensive; merchants know retention is the answer but don't have a system.
3. **Experience vs. discount trap:** Bad product experience is the #1 reason customers leave — merchants default to discounts instead of fixing the experience.
4. **Personalization hurdle:** Customers want personalized loyalty but hesitate to share data; merchants struggle to get the data they need.
5. **Program sameness:** 80% of executives admit their loyalty program looks like competitors (PwC) — merchants don't know how to differentiate.

### What This Article MUST Achieve
- Give Shopify merchants a clear, executable framework for building a loyalty program — not generic theory
- Ground every step in real merchant pain: high CAC, discount dependency, one-time buyer conversion
- Show Joy Loyalty as the execution layer — not a pitch, but the natural tool that makes each step actionable
- Differentiate from competitor articles by including: ecommerce-specific tactics, Joy merchant examples where available, and the emotional loyalty angle competitors only mention superficially

## G/ Verified Stats — USE THESE

All stats below are from PwC's primary survey: *"Building customer loyalty"* — 410 executives (surveyed Oct 15–Nov 22, 2022) + 4,036 consumers (surveyed May 2022).
**Source:** https://www.pwc.com/us/en/services/consulting/business-transformation/library/building-customer-loyalty-guide.html

### The Loyalty Confidence Gap (Core Hook)
- **61%** of executives say customers are MORE loyal than before the pandemic
- But only **20%** of consumers say the same
- **75%** of consumers report no change in their loyalty

### Why Customers Leave
- **37%** of consumers cite bad product/service experience as the reason they left a brand
- **32%** cite bad customer service experience
- (Executives agree: 41% cite price increases, 37% cite bad product experience)

### How Loyalty is Actually Built (Consumer perspective)
- **46%** of consumers say loyalty is determined by using the product and liking the quality
- Only **11%** say good customer service determines loyalty (lower than executives assume)

### How Consumers Show Loyalty
- **60%** show loyalty by recommending to friends/family — **word of mouth is the #1 loyalty signal**
- **43%** subscribe to products/services
- **37%** participate in a loyalty program
- **9%** only buy from one brand (exclusive loyalty is rare)

### Program Landscape
- **70%** of businesses have a formal loyalty program
- **63%** increased their loyalty program budget in the latest planning cycle
- **91%** of executives agree loyalty programs should provide more rewards/benefits
- **80%** say their program looks similar to competitors' — the sameness problem is real

### Budget
- Average loyalty budget: ~**5% of company revenue**

### Personalization Gap
- **73%** of executives collect email addresses for personalization; only **61%** of consumers are willing to share it
- **32%** of executives collect location data; only **10%** of consumers are willing to share it
- **48%** of consumers most value **discounts/rebates** as a personalized loyalty benefit
- **43%** value **flexible loyalty rewards** — flexibility matters more than novelty
