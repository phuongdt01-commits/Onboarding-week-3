## D/ Main Intent & User Context

### Search intent

**Primary:** Informational/educational — understand what a channel loyalty platform is, how it works, and what features matter when evaluating one.

**Secondary:**
- **Commercial investigation** — comparing platform providers and their specific capabilities
- **Implementation** — seeking step-by-step frameworks for building a channel loyalty program
- **Problem-solving** — diagnosing why current programs fail and how to fix them

### User context (ICP) = Core user problems

The searcher is likely a:
- **B2B brand manager or channel marketing lead** evaluating loyalty platforms for their partner network (distributors, resellers, dealers)
- **Ecommerce brand owner** exploring whether channel loyalty concepts (affiliate, wholesale, influencer partnerships) apply to their business
- **Marketing operations person** tasked with replacing manual spreadsheet-based partner incentives with automated systems

**Core problems:**
1. High partner acquisition costs — cheaper to retain existing channel partners than find new ones
2. Operational friction — manual tracking, delayed reward payments, spreadsheet chaos erode partner trust
3. Lack of visibility — can't see real-time performance data across regions or partner tiers
4. Low partner mindshare — struggling to stay top-of-mind when partners work with competing vendors
5. Program complexity — systems too hard for partners to understand or engage with

### What this article MUST achieve

1. **Define clearly** — what a channel loyalty platform is and how it differs from consumer/B2C loyalty
2. **Map incentive structures** — SPIFFs, MDFs, rebates, deal registration, tiered programs explained with examples
3. **Show platform capabilities** — AI, real-time analytics, automation, API integration as must-have features
4. **Provide implementation framework** — actionable steps from goal-setting to launch to optimization
5. **Address failure prevention** — why programs fail + how to avoid each pitfall
6. **Demonstrate ROI** — actual KPIs, formulas, and benchmarks (not just "increases revenue")
7. **Bridge to ecommerce** — show how Shopify brands can apply channel loyalty (affiliates, wholesale, influencer networks)

### Key Terminology to Define
- **SPIFFs** — Sales Performance Incentive Funds (short-term, rep-level)
- **MDFs** — Market Development Funds (front-end, proposal-based marketing support)
- **Co-Op Funds** — rebates based on prior sales (back-end incentives)
- **Deal Registration** — exclusive/preferential rights to work a claimed sales opportunity
- **Point Economy** — points-based earning/redemption system
- **Tier Multipliers** — bonus earning rates at higher partner levels
- **CDP** — Customer Data Platform (unified partner data)
- **PRM** — Partner Relationship Management (partner portal systems)
- **Breadth vs Depth** — expand reach (new partners) vs deepen value (existing partners)
