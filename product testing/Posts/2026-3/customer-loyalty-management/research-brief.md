## **8 Core Factors (APPROVED)**

### **Factor 1: Program Type Selection**
*Foundational, supportive*

**What to cover:**
- Five loyalty program archetypes: Tiered, Points-based, Spend-based, Subscription, Value-based
- How to choose the right model for your store (revenue stage, customer behavior)
- When to evolve from one model to another

**Key Examples:**
- Tiered: Starbucks (Green/Gold tiers), Amazon Prime (subscription-based)
- Points-based: Chipotle, Sephora
- Value-based: TOMS (philanthropic rewards)

**Verified Stats:**
- ✅ 5–25x acquisition cost vs retention (Harvard Business Review, 2014)
- ✅ 75% of customers want rewards for non-transactional activity (Loyalty360)

---

### **Factor 2: Reward Design Beyond Transactions**
*Operational, primary*

**What to cover:**
- Points for actions, not just purchases: reviews, referrals, social shares, email signups
- Experiential rewards vs. monetary rewards
- Behavior incentives that drive repeat purchases (double-point days, limited-time multipliers)
- Balance short-term and long-term redemption paths

**Key Tactics:**
- Immediate welcome bonus for first-time members
- Birthday/anniversary offers
- Win-back campaigns (dormant customer reactivation)
- Reward breadth: encourage multiple engagement types, not just spending

**Verified Stats:**
- ✅ 89% of consumers trust friends/family recommendations (Ipsos via Marketing Charts) — supports referral incentive importance
- ✅ 264% year-over-year lift in loyalty revenue (Pace Athletic via Yotpo case study) — demonstrates behavior-based reward ROI
- ✅ 25% conversion rate increase with incentive optimization (Perry Ellis via Yotpo case study)

---

### **Factor 3: Customer Segmentation & Personalization**
*Operational, primary*

**What to cover:**
- Why one-size-fits-all loyalty fails (different customers want different rewards)
- Behavioral segmentation: high-spenders, frequent purchasers, dormant, new members
- Campaign personalization: birthday rewards, anniversary offers, win-back flows
- VIP tiers (exclusive benefits for top-tier customers)
- Predictive segmentation using first-party data

**Key Tactics:**
- Segment by purchase frequency, order value, last purchase date
- Tailor reward types per segment (high-spender prefers status/exclusivity; new member prefers quick wins)
- Automate win-back campaigns for churn-risk customers

**Verified Stats:**
- ✅ 10x loyalty participation growth and 3.4x higher CLV for redeemers (MicroPerfumes via Rivo case study)
- ✅ 3.5x higher repeat purchase rate for loyalty members (MicroPerfumes via Rivo case study)

---

### **Factor 4: Analytics & ROI Measurement**
*Operational, primary*

**What to cover:**
- Key performance indicators: LTV, AOV, Redemption Rate, Churn Rate, Repeat Purchase Rate, CAC payback period
- Building loyalty dashboards: What to track, how to visualize, how to communicate to stakeholders
- Proving program value to executives: cost of program vs. incremental revenue generated
- A/B testing loyalty mechanics (welcome bonus amount, tier thresholds, reward types)
- Assisted Orders metric (Joy-specific north star)

**Key Tactics:**
- Track redemption rates per reward type (reveals which incentives drive behavior)
- Compare loyalty member LTV vs. non-member LTV (quantify member value)
- Set realistic ROI benchmarks (year 1 = slow, year 2+ = compound growth)
- Measure engagement beyond dollars: repeat purchase frequency, review submission rate, referral conversions

**Verified Stats:**
- ✅ Redeemers spend $637 more than non-redeemers annually (Pace Athletic case study)
- ✅ 70% higher repeat purchase rate for loyalty redeemers vs. non-redeemers (Pace Athletic case study)
- ✅ 112% increase in reviews volume from loyalty integration (Jane Iredale via Yotpo case study)

---

### **Factor 5: Omnichannel Integration**
*Operational, primary*

**What to cover:**
- Loyalty consistency across channels: web, mobile app, email, SMS, POS (in-store)
- Syncing points/tier status in real-time across channels
- Mobile app as loyalty hub: where members check balance, discover rewards, redeem
- Email as communication channel: personalized offers, redemption reminders, tier achievement notifications
- SMS integration: time-sensitive offers, win-back campaigns
- Technical challenges: data fragmentation, API integration, customer identity resolution

**Key Tactics:**
- Unify customer data (avoid siloed points or duplicate accounts)
- Enable cross-channel redemption (earn online, redeem in-store; vice versa)
- Use mobile as primary discovery channel for mobile-first audiences
- Automate email/SMS flows based on lifecycle stage and behavior

**Verification Note:** ⚠️ Multi-channel claims (250% engagement, 22% LTV, 15–25% revenue from Rivo) are secondary aggregations. Not advised for primary citation; instead: "Brands using 3+ loyalty channels report higher engagement" or find original sources (MoldStud, World Metrics, Envive.ai).

---

### **Factor 6: Frictionless Onboarding**
*Operational, primary*

**What to cover:**
- Signup friction kills loyalty: simplify with one-click registration, social login, guest checkout option
- Immediate value: welcome bonus must appear before or during signup, not after
- First-member experience: How to educate new members on rewards while removing complexity
- Mobile-first onboarding: Not all traffic is desktop
- Progressive enrollment: Collect data over time, not all upfront

**Key Tactics:**
- One-click signup option (pre-fill email if logged in)
- Instant welcome discount or bonus points at signup
- Show tier progression path immediately (visual ladder to aspirational tier)
- Automate first reward redemption email (remove friction)
- Test signup flow for mobile usability

**Verified Stats:**
- ✅ 75% want rewards for non-transactional activity — frictionless onboarding removes the barrier to *trying* the program first (Loyalty360)

---

### **Factor 7: AI & Predictive Analytics**
*Operational/Trends, emerging*

**What to cover:**
- Churn prediction: Using historical behavior to identify at-risk customers before they leave
- Personalization automation: AI recommends which reward type per customer segment
- Win-back automation: Predictive triggers for re-engagement campaigns
- Sentiment analysis: AI reads customer feedback to refine rewards
- Future-proofing: Why AI literacy matters for loyalty managers now

**Key Trends:**
- Predictive analytics reduce manual segmentation work
- Behavioral AI adapts reward recommendations in real-time
- Automation scales personalization beyond what humans can manage

**Strategic Note:** AI implementation is mentioned across SERP but rarely with *step-by-step how-to*. Joy can differentiate here by showing **specific workflows**: "Set up a churn prediction model in 3 steps", "automate win-back campaigns using behavior triggers", etc.

**Verification Note:** SERP sources mention AI as trend but lack specifics. No verified case studies yet for AI loyalty implementations. Frame as "emerging" and focus on *why* (reduces manual work, improves targeting precision).

---

### **Factor 8: Feedback Loops & Continuous Optimization**
*Operational, primary*

**What to cover:**
- Exit surveys: Why members quit (essential for churn reduction)
- Post-redemption feedback: Do members actually *want* the rewards they earn?
- A/B testing rewards: Which tier structure, point value, or reward type drives higher engagement?
- Quarterly optimization cycles: Don't set loyalty on autopilot
- Closed-loop feedback: Learn from data, test hypothesis, iterate

**Key Tactics:**
- Deploy exit surveys when members downgrade or become inactive
- Send post-redemption NPS: "Was this reward valuable?" (guides future reward design)
- Run A/B tests on: tier thresholds, point values, reward types, email send times
- Schedule monthly reviews of key metrics (redemption rate, repeat purchase lift, CAC payback)

**Verified Stats:**
- ✅ Program stagnation is a core user problem — continuous optimization directly addresses this
