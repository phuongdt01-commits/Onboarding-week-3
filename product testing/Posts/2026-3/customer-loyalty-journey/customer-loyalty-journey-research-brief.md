**Topic:** Customer Loyalty Journey
**Funnel Stage:** Solution Aware

## Core Factors (7) — SERP Consensus

1. **Journey Stages** — Structured progression through awareness → enrollment → engagement → retention → advocacy
2. **Personalization at Scale** — Tailored experiences across all touchpoints and customer segments
3. **Recognition & Status Systems** — Tiered benefits, customer recognition, status hierarchies
4. **Non-Transactional Earning** — Engagement activities beyond purchases (community, referrals, reviews, social)
5. **Measurement & ROI Metrics** — NPS, retention rate, CLV, engagement score, customer loyalty index
6. **Community Building** — Creating belonging and "tribe" mentality; customers want to be part of something
7. **Multi-Touchpoint Optimization** — Recognizing customers across all interaction paths, not just transactions
