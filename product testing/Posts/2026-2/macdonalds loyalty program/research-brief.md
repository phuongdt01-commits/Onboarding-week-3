## Brand Examples

### Starbucks Rewards — Coffee / QSR
- **Mechanic:** Stars per dollar spent; tiered redemption; personalized app offers; mobile order-and-pay (31% of transactions). Gamified Double Star Days.
- **Result:** 34.6M active U.S. members; members drive ~59% of U.S. store sales; 3x higher spend per visit vs. non-members; 44% retention rate (nearly 2x industry average)
- **Source:** https://www.myhubble.money/blogs/how-34-6m-active-members-drive-59-of-sales-for-starbucks

### Taco Bell — QSR / Mexican Fast Food
- **Mechanic:** Points-based app loyalty + $10/month subscription ("Taco Lover's Pass" — one free taco/day). App-exclusive deals drive enrollment.
- **Result:** Subscription generated 20% net new rewards members; visit frequency jumped from 5.8 → 10.2 visits/year post-enrollment (76% lift)
- **Source:** https://www.restaurantdive.com/news/taco-bells-subscription-service-increased-rewards-members-by-20/619254/

### Domino's Piece of the Pie Rewards — Pizza / QSR
- **Mechanic:** 10 points per eligible $10+ order; 60 points = free medium pizza. First national pizza chain to offer loyalty across all order channels.
- **Result:** 25 million active members by 2019; members represent 25%+ of 85M-customer database
- **Source:** https://brainstation.io/magazine/dominos-digital-loyalty-program-tops-25-million-members *(2019 stat — use with year qualifier)*

### Burger King Royal Perks — QSR Burger
- **Mechanic:** Points per dollar via app; tiered crowns system (Royal → King); personalized offers and limited-time challenges
- **Result:** 30M active members worldwide; 900% increase in app downloads since launch; 12% improvement in customer retention
- **Source:** https://coolest-gadgets.com/burger-king-statistics/
