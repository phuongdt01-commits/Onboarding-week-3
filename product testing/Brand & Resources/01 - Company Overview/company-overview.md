# Joy Loyalty — Company Overview

## About Joy

Joy is a loyalty platform built for Shopify merchants who are serious about customer retention. It was founded in 2021 and originally operated as a Magento enterprise loyalty tech partner before pivoting to Shopify to serve a broader, faster-growing merchant market.

## Core Focus

Joy's business is built around one core idea: **it's cheaper and more valuable to retain existing customers than to acquire new ones.**

- Sell more to repeated customers
- Reduce churn through loyalty mechanics
- Drive repeat purchases through points, tiers, VIP programs, and referrals

---

## North-Star Metric

> **Number of Assisted Orders**

An "Assisted Order" is any repeat purchase that was influenced or enabled by Joy's loyalty program. This is the primary metric Joy tracks — not signups, not redemptions — but actual revenue-generating repeat purchases.

## Brand Story

> **Built with retention experts. Built for max flexibility.**

The very first version of Joy was released in 2021 by a group of experienced eCommerce experts. For years, the team stood as a technology partner to medium-to-large enterprises, building robust loyalty solutions on Magento. They witnessed first-hand what true scale and flexibility looked like.

As Shopify emerged as the platform of choice for the next generation of ambitious brands, they recognized a critical gap: fast-growing merchants faced persistent technological limitations in building the truly unique, branded reward programs they envisioned.

Joy was built to empower brands to grow big and sustainably — especially those that dare to dream big and stand out. Joy Loyalty was born to bring **enterprise-grade power and flexibility to Shopify**, transforming loyalty from a mere feature into a measurable, indispensable engine for growth.
