# Joy Competitive Landscape — 2026 Update

> **Source:** Internal competitor analysis document (H2 2025 review)
> **Direct competitors:** Smile, Rivo
> **Emerging threats:** Toki, Casa

---

## State of Play — H2 2025

### Smile
- Still holding the #1 position on Shopify app store, but showing signs of slowing down
- **Warning:** Smile has returned focus to Shopify — the sleeping giant may be waking up
- Fewer significant product updates in recent periods
- High pricing; less innovative
- Strong brand presence, video guides, and US market dominance

### Rivo
- Still a major and dangerous competitor; product updates are frequent
- **Signs of crisis:** 1-star reviews increasing, rating dropped to 4.8, lost Built for Shopify (BFS) badge
- Reviews are no longer growing — count has been declining
- Shifting acquisition strategy: less reliance on app store distribution, focusing on Social and other channels
- Strong developer-first positioning and technical depth

### BON (Vietnam)
- Unexpected growth in reviews and ranking — briefly hit #1 on app store before Smile reclaimed it
- Aggressive review acquisition strategy
- **However:** Product has not improved much; design and features not strong enough to move upmarket
- **Warning for Joy:** Monitor both marketing/reviews AND actual product quality simultaneously

### Joy
- Significant progress in reconstruction phase
- Rule engine improved, API expanded, new JS SDK
- Team more focused — especially on UI/UX aesthetics and customization → winning more Plus and near-Plus deals
- Video guides added
- **Key pricing adjustment:** VIP tier moved from $24.99 → $99, targeting mid-market and above. Improved ARPU and eliminated support cost/revenue mismatch
- Need to continue accelerating to catch Rivo and capitalize on Smile's slow period

## Joy's Competitive Advantages

### 1. Avada Ecosystem
Joy benefits from being part of the Avada app ecosystem — a network of 10+ Shopify apps with cross-sell, bundle, and partnership capabilities. Competitors like Smile and Rivo are standalone; Toki and Casa are still building ecosystems.

> "Avada ecosystem = a moat that competitors want to copy but can't easily."

### 2. Pricing Efficiency
Joy operates with lower overhead than US/UK competitors due to geographic advantage. This means:
- More competitive pricing without cutting margins
- Ability to offer more at lower price points
- Better ARPU from pricing adjustment ($24.99 → $99 for VIP tier)

### 3. Geographic Advantage
- Strong in Asia (home market)
- US and EU are growth markets; competitors are weaker in Asia/APAC
- Japan + South Korea + APAC + Australia + key EU markets (Germany, France, UK) together approach the size of the US market

### 4. Product Parity
- Joy's product quality is now on par with Smile and Rivo
- Rule engine improved significantly; API and SDK complete
- Win rate for Plus and near-Plus merchants increasing

## Key Strategic Lessons from H2 2025

1. **Reviews ≠ Product quality** — BON proved you can grow reviews fast, but it won't work upmarket without real product improvement
2. **Competitors never sleep** — Rivo is very aggressive; Smile has refocused on Shopify
3. **Balance is everything** — Product quality + Speed + Marketing/Reviews + Support
4. **Branding changes need careful consideration** — The "Love" app dropped from rank 5 to 16 after a branding change to black; branding affects ranking perception
5. **Free models are becoming dangerous weapons** — Toki (freemium) and Casa (fully free) are growing very fast
6. **Speed to market is critical** — New players like Toki and Casa built complete features in 6–24 months

## 2026 Competitive Strategy

**The real competition is now about people, not product.**

> Joy's product is on par with competitors. The 3 differentiators that will win in 2026:
> 1. **Support** — Deeper loyalty expertise, proactive not reactive
> 2. **Human Resources** — Large team trained to be developers, not just coders
> 3. **Team capability** — Close the English-language and builder-mindset gaps

**Timeline:**
- **2025** — Reconstruction: rebuild product foundation, optimize pricing, improve infrastructure ✓
- **2026** — Compete and innovate: leverage people advantage, push ahead of Rivo, capture Smile's dormant market share
