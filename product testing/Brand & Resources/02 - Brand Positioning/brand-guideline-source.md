## Brand Strategy

## Who we are

Joy loyalty is a loyalty platform built for Shopify fast-growing and ambitious merchants. Joy loyalty focuses on **maximizing customer retention** & ROI through building **rewarding customer relationship & optimize customer acquisition cost (CAC)** 

**Ultimate goal of Joy loyalty**: Sell more to repeated customers = Have more repeated orders

- [North-Star Metric:  **Number of Assisted Orders**: Orders placed through referral links hoặc using loyalty-generated discount codes](https://www.notion.so/20eb0da449f180aca636c1069b854662?pvs=21).
1. **Brand mission**
    
    To empower the world’s most ambitious e-commerce brands to achieve absolute mastery over customer retention, turning every buyer into a loyal advocate and market leader.
    
2. **Brand promise**
    
    Enable brands to design, build, or integrate their loyalty programs whatever way they desire

1. **Target audience**
    
    **An ambitious & driven-minded Shopify store owner**
    
    **About**: They desire building an outstanding leading brand. They are ambitious and confident. They’re aspired to become number one, to become a market leader in their industry. They hate to be an ordinary unmemorable disappointing brand. They are analytical and put a great care into statistics like customer retention, ROI and CAC. 
    
    **Industries:** `Beauty & fitness` `Apparel` `Sports & Outdoor` `Nutrition & Supplements` `Pet care`
    
    **Location:** `Asia` `USA` `EU`
    
    **Merchant size**: Mid-Enterprise
    
    **Firmography:**
    
    - **Monthly revenue**: $50K-500K (sweet spot cho loyalty investment)
    - **Ad spend**: $10K+ monthly (feeling acquisition cost pressure)
    - **Order volume**: 400+ orders/month (approaching or at Shopify Plus level)
    
    **Pains**:
    
    - **Unsustainable high CAC**: They are spending too much on acquiring new customers while customer retention is still low
    - **Low customer retention**: They fear that customers do not come back.
    - **Generic & unmemorable loyalty programs**: The fear of having a loyalty program that is no difference from their competitors
    - **Technical limitation**: Being limited by other loyalty apps that prevents them from executing complex, multi-tiered, or hyper-personalized programs necessary for a Mid-Enterprise brand
    - **Reactive, Non-Strategic Support:** Current support is focused only on troubleshooting. They need a partner who acts as a strategic coach, providing proactive, goal-oriented advice on how to tweak their program to improve Retention/ROI, not just fix bugs.
    
    **Needs**:
    
    - **Absolute control**: They need a platform that grants them the power and **flexibility** to **design, build, and integrate** rewards *exactly on their own terms*, without technical barriers.
    - **Limitless configuration**: The ability to execute intricate loyalty logic, such as highly segmented VIP tiers, custom multi-currency rewards, or brand-specific rules
    - **Clear, superior ROI**: Advanced reports to show revenues from assisted orders, ROI, CLV, customer retention
    - **An outstanding brand**: They need to become a memorable brand that stands out from their competitors
2. **Brand story**
    
    **Built with retention experts - Built for max flexibility**
    
    The very first version of Joy was released in 2021 by a group of experienced & enthusiastic **experts in eCommerce**. For years, we stood as a technology partner to medium-sized to big enterprises, building robust loyalty solutions on Magento. We saw first-hand what true scale and flexibility looked like. As **Shopify** emerged as the platform of choice for **the next generation of ambitious brands**, we recognized **a critical gap**: these fast-growing merchants faced persistent technological **limitations** in building the **truly unique, branded reward programs** they envisioned.
    
    This wasn't just about porting a solution; it was about addressing a fundamental need. We desired to build a solution that **empowers brands to grow big and sustainably**, especially those that **dare to dream big and stand out from the crowd**. Joy Loyalty was born from this conviction: to bring **enterprise-grade power and flexibility** to **Shopify**, transforming loyalty from a mere feature into a **measurable**, **indispensable** engine for **growth.**

    Joy app arms **ambitious** and **fast-growing Shopify brands** with the **most powerful** and **flexible loyalty platform,** built to maximize retention.

Our app** is truly for brands who desire to outperform competitors, maximize retention, and crush ROI targets.

            Characteristic breakdown:
            
            | **Foresighted** | Anticipate future challenges & opportunities, looking several steps ahead rather than focusing only on the present |
            | --- | --- |
            | **Goal-Driven** | Every action is tethered to a clear, overarching objective or vision |
            | **Analytical** | use data and systems thinking to understand how different components interact, identify root causes, and evaluate the potential consequences of various paths |
            | **Proactive** | design plans and develop contingency options *before* an event occurs, minimizing reactive responses. |
            | **Adaptable** | treat their plans as flexible roadmaps, ready to pivot or adjust the strategy when new information or unexpected changes arise. |

    3.         **Brand voice**   
        
        *“To empower the world’s most ambitious e-commerce brands to achieve absolute mastery over customer relationships, turning every buyer into a loyal advocate and market leader.”*
        
    
    | **Tone Element** | **Description** | **Execution Example** |
    | --- | --- | --- |
    | **Strategic/Authoritative** | Always links features back to high-level business outcomes. Focuses on the "why" and "what if." |  |
    | **Motivational** | Inspires the customer to conquer their business challenges (CAC, low retention). Challenges the status quo. |  |
    | **Direct & High-Paced** | Avoids lengthy preambles. Uses punchy, active verbs and short sentences suitable for busy, driven executives. | "We arm ambitious brands. We deliver power. We guarantee control." |
    | **Confident** | Asserts leadership and certainty. Speaks with authority and certainty about our capability and the customer's eventual success. | "Joy **is** the definitive choice." / "You will **crush** your ROI targets." |
    - **Strategic** (*Foresighted, goal-driven, analytical, proactive*):
        
        
        | DOs | DON’Ts |
        | --- | --- |
        | Always back features, insights, opinions with statistics, trust-worthy researches, analytics, or reports
        *eg: “48% increase in customer lifetime value encouraged by rewards”* | Use general words or claim generic statements without proof, evidence,  |
        | Always connect app’s features/updates with the vision/mission/north-star metrics (customer retention). Emphasize the purpose
        *eg: “Unlock repeat purchases and growth with Memberships”; “Total mastery over CAC with flexible referral programs”…*  | Only describe features technically |
        | Prioritize easy to understand languages & expression while staying 
        Use vocabulary that target audience is already familiar with  | Use lengthy & academic terms. Presume that audience will already understand |
    - **Motivational** (*inspirational, ambitious,  Enthusiastic, Passionate, Encouraging, Uplifting, Empowering, Galvanizing, Stimulating, Rousing, Energizing*):
        
        A motivational voice encourages and inspires the audience to take action and achieve their goals. It uses positive, energetic language to boost confidence and drive.
        
        | DOs | DON’Ts |
        | --- | --- |
        | Always include the most potential possible outcomes that customers can achieve. Focus on the **transformation**
        eg: *“**No more restrictions**. Build whatever loyalty programs for your bands. Now!”;  “**2x repeated purchases** with diversified reward programs” ; “Now your customers can ….”* | Avoid talking about statistics of possible results or taking lightly about what customers can achieve |
        | Believe in **a better world**. Use **strong, positive statements** that motivate actions |  |
        | Focus on telling **heroic & successful stories**. Use motif: clear villian (challenge/struggles..) + belief in victory (solution) + a clear success of triumph/transformation |  |
    - **Direct & high-paced** (*Straightforward*, *clear, concise, prices, uncomplicated, unambiguous, no-nonsense)*
        
        A direct voice is straightforward, clear, and unambiguous, focusing on concise and precise communication. It avoids unnecessary complexity or embellishment, aiming to convey information in the most efficient manner possible.
        
        | DOs | DON’Ts |
        | --- | --- |
        | Cut all unnecessary words, flowery language and long explanations. It should be quick to read and understand | Avoid lengthy sentences or expression. Prioritize shorter versions of explanation or statements |
        | Focus on command & action. Use strong verbs and imperative sentences that tell the audience exactly what to do, eliminating hesitation. 
        Eg: *“Stop imagining. Start executing your unique loyalty programs with Joy now”* |  |
        | It is encouraged to use **bolding**, CAPITAL letters, short paragraphs and clear bullet points |  |
        | It is encouraged to use exclamation points, dashes, and periods aggressively when needed |  |
    - **Confident** (*Authoritative, trustworthy, Decisive, Influential, Positive, Hopeful, Upbeat)*
        
        A confident voice is positive, hopeful, and focused on favorable outcomes. It emphasizes opportunities, progress, and the potential for positive change, even in challenging situations.
        
    
    | DOs | DON’Ts |
    | --- | --- |
    | Use strong, active verbs and state facts clearly. Eg: *“Joy delivers the most flexible and powerful loyalty platform among other competitors”* | Use conditional or tentative language (e.g., *might, maybe, perhaps, we think, we hope*) |
    | Focus on results and guarantees. Highlight our mastery, successful results (come with statistics) | Over promise about what we are not yet able to deliver |
    | **Tone:** Poised, composed, direct, and slightly authoritative. The pace should be steady—not rushed, not hesitant. | Be too loud, too braggy or agressive about Joy’s achievements.  |
    | Use inclusive language (”us”, “together”, “we”) to show we are a partner. *Eg. “Succeed together”. “Rising together”* |  |
    
    **d. Brand volabulary & expression**
    
    - **Power & Victory:** Crush, Outperform, Conquer, Dominate, Weapon, Arsenal, Unrestricted, Superior, Definitive, Victory**.**
    - **Control & Expertise:** Mastery, Command, Strategic, Battle-tested, Limitless, Configure, Relentless, Goal-oriented.
    - **Results & Metrics:** ROI, Revenue, Assisted Orders, Maximize, Multiply, Lifetime Value (CLV), Performance. Gather trustworthy statistics and mention as much as possible to back Joy’s USPs.
