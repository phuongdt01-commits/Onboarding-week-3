# Joy Loyalty — Pricing Structure

## Overview

Joy's pricing is designed to serve both mass-market merchants and enterprise brands, with a clear upgrade path tied to program complexity.

**Key 2025 Pricing Update:** VIP Tier feature moved from $24.99 plan → $99 plan. This better matches support costs to revenue and positions Joy clearly in the mid-market and above.

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Basic (~$25/month)
**Target:** Merchant starting with loyalty or with simpler programs

**Included:**
- Points program (diverse earning formats)
- Referrals
- Pre-built integrations (full access)
- Analytics (basic)
- Loyalty touchpoints (widget, loyalty page)
- Standard support

**Best for:** Stores with up to ~400 orders/month that want core loyalty mechanics at a reasonable price

---

### Advanced ($99/month)
**Target:** Mid-market merchants who want to differentiate their loyalty program

**Included (on top of Basic):**
- VIP Tier program (full customization)
- Rule Engine (bonus campaigns, custom logic)
- Priority customization support
- Advanced analytics and reporting

**Best for:** $50K–$200K/month revenue stores that need tiered loyalty and rule-based automations

---

### Enterprise ($499/month)
**Target:** High-volume Shopify Plus merchants and brands requiring full enterprise control

**Included (on top of Advanced):**
- Omnichannel sync
- Checkout Extension (Shopify Plus)
- Full API & webhooks access
- B2B tier system
- Data sync to external platforms (Klaviyo segments, etc.)
- Dedicated Account Manager
- Custom feature development on request
- Strategic consultation (Product Team / CTO)
- Priority issue resolution
- Data migration support from other platforms

**Best for:** $200K–$500K+/month stores, Shopify Plus, enterprise brands with technical teams or complex multi-channel needs

## Revenue Contribution

Joy's merchant base is roughly split:
- **~50%** revenue from Mass & Mid-market customers
- **~50%** revenue from High-volume & Enterprise customers

This balance reflects Joy's dual-track strategy: accessible for growing brands, powerful enough for enterprise.
