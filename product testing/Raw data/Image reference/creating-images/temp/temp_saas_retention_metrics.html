5 key SaaS retention metrics to track
CRR
Customer Retention Rate
% of accounts still active at end of period. Your baseline logo retention number.
Logo
GRR
Gross Revenue Retention
Revenue held onto excluding expansion. Always ≤100%. Healthy floor: 80–85%+.
Floor
NRR
Net Revenue Retention
Revenue from existing customers including expansion minus churn & downgrades. Can exceed 100% — meaning growth from existing customers alone. Target: 110%+ for growth-stage SaaS.
Can exceed 100%
CLV / LTV
Customer Lifetime Value
Total revenue per customer over their lifespan. Improves as retention increases.
Revenue
Churn Rate
Monthly & Annual Churn
Inverse of retention. 2% monthly = ~22% annual churn. Track both timeframes.
Inverse
