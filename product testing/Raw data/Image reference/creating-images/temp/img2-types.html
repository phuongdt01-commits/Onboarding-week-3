Types of customer service operations

1
Reactive Support
Responds to inbound requests. Help desks, call centers, email, live chat.
2
Proactive Support
Reaches customers before problems. Alerts, reminders, notifications.
3
Self-Service
Customers resolve issues independently. Knowledge bases, FAQs, chatbots.
4
Omnichannel
Support across all channels with unified customer history.
5
Tiered Support
Matches problem complexity with agent expertise. Tier 1, 2, 3.
6
Outsourced vs In-house
Hybrid approach: complex cases internal, volume outsourced.
